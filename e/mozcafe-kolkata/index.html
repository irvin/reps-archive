Agenda For the Meetup:-

  • Creation of a formal Mozilla community in Kolkata to serve as a base for all volunteers as well as anyone interested in contributing to Mozilla from Kolkata and the surrounding regions.

  • Recruiting new volunteers/community members.

  • Organizing Mozilla events in Kolkata and deciding upon tentative dates and venues for these.

  • Getting more students involved in FOSS related activities in Kolkata.

  • Interacting with other FOSS / Tech groups to spread greater FOSS awareness in the Eastern parts of India.

People Turnout: 30

Outcomes From The Meetup:-

  • Creation of Mozilla Kolkata Community.

  • Identifying key areas of improvements for the local community.

  • Identified potential venues for future events in the area. Namely:-

    • Bengal Engineering & Science University, Shibpur;
    • Netaji Subhas Engineering College;
    • Techno India College;
    • St.Xavier's College;
    • Dr. B.C. Roy College, Durgapur.

  • Formal Discussion with the local FOSS groups resulted in a lot of great ideas, including:-

    • Creation of a common events calendar for all local FOSS communities to share plan out their events in collaboration with each other;
    • Planning out incentives for teachers/mentors from various institutes who promote FOSS activities among the students in the region;
    • Planning out various competition-type online/offline events for students in the area to keep interest levels high.

Future Events Planning:-

Become a Community Coordinator in Your Region!

The Mozilla Reps program is open to all Mozillians who are 18 years of age and above. Before you become a Mozilla Rep, you must follow our learning process so that you can become a good Community Coordinator in your region.

Community Coordinators are not a power structure over other Mozillians, instead they are here to help, empower and motivate other Mozillians to contribute to Mozilla projects.

There are tons of opportunities you can benefit from joining this program:

  • Access to a global community which includes people from many different countries and cultural backgrounds.
  • Direct support from Mozilla to help you empower others in your region to advance Mozilla’s goals.
  • Opportunity to help your local community grow.
  • Grow your leadership and communication skills and build networks with new people from many different countries around the globe.
  • Amplify Mozilla’s impact in your local region and functional area.

If you want to learn more about our program, you might want to connect with one of our currently active Mozilla Reps in your region, so you can learn and understand the challenges and opportunities of this program.
