Description RIW 2014 - the main autumn event in Russia which consists of three parts (Internet, Media and Telecom), bringing together the conference, media communication forum, exhibition and many other activities like shows, presentations and promo-actions.

The conference includes all the main directions of development of the Internet, Media and Telecom business, technology, regulation, government initiatives, trends and prospects.

A number of major themes - represented by individual unit-conferences:

  • Advertisement
  • Analytics and Trends of Communication Media industry
  • social Media
  • Mobile technology, development and services
  • Web Development
  • project Management
  • Training and education
  • Information Security
  • Legal regulation and government initiatives
  • media convergence
  • Telecom and so on. (more than 10 pack-conferences)

The event brings together experts and recognized experts of IT-industry and the media sphere, officers of the Russian and foreign companies, government officials, representatives of the relevant ministries and agencies, journalists, and ordinary Internet users.

The exhibition will feature more than 150 exhibitors, 8 conference rooms for all three days of the conference will be run in parallel, and a block of the conference on actual problems of development of Russian Internet, according to the Organizing Committee of the number of speakers RIW 2014 will be more than 600 people, professional participants - more than 4 thousand. Man three days of the exhibition and forum will visit more than 20 thousand. participants.

Become a Community Coordinator in Your Region!

The Mozilla Reps program is open to all Mozillians who are 18 years of age and above. Before you become a Mozilla Rep, you must follow our learning process so that you can become a good Community Coordinator in your region.

Community Coordinators are not a power structure over other Mozillians, instead they are here to help, empower and motivate other Mozillians to contribute to Mozilla projects.

There are tons of opportunities you can benefit from joining this program:

  • Access to a global community which includes people from many different countries and cultural backgrounds.
  • Direct support from Mozilla to help you empower others in your region to advance Mozilla’s goals.
  • Opportunity to help your local community grow.
  • Grow your leadership and communication skills and build networks with new people from many different countries around the globe.
  • Amplify Mozilla’s impact in your local region and functional area.

If you want to learn more about our program, you might want to connect with one of our currently active Mozilla Reps in your region, so you can learn and understand the challenges and opportunities of this program.

  • The applicant needs to be at least 18 years old
